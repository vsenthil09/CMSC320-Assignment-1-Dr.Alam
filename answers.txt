Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data Science can be applied to the field of finance, an example of how it can help is by 
analyzing market data and finance statements, and using that to assess risk levels. This can help 
Financial firms make smarter choices and better investment decisions. This overall will help the 
companies manage financial risk, which will in turn help their company grow.
